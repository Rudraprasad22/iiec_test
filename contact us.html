About Us

The Entrepreneurship Cell (E-Cell) is a non-profit organization run by the students of IISER Berhampur. This is an initiative to bring together a group of remarkable people to bring up the entrepreneurial spirit in the student community and stimulate the metamorphosis of gleaming ideas into real-world businesses.
